ABOUT ME

Hi! I am glad that you visited my personal website. 😊
My name is Aleksandra Timofejeva, I am from Latvia, Riga. I am pursuing my B.Eng.Sc. (last year student).
I'm a passionate learner and a detail-oriented person. What I love the most - applying theoretical knowledge to practice, creating things and seeing how they work, thinking about how to improve them.
Programming languages I am mostly interested in are JavaScript, Python and C# .NET. In addition to coding, I believe design and usability plays an important role in software, that is why I am also improving my skills in UI and UX design. From University I have gained knowledge about Electronics, microcontroller programming, Computer-aided design and some other Robotics-specific subjects.
Besides programming, I really enjoy learning about space and space technology. I love reading, nature and admiring vastness and beauty of the universe. ⭐
